Creating functional products with beautiful design is my passion.

I’m Brian Warchesik and I’m a User Experience Designer based in Colorado that loves to code.

Combining technology, research, and aesthetics is a highly rewarding experience for me. The ability to speak the languages of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript has allowed me to bridge the gap between art, design, and science. When done right, UX design combined with frontend development is itself, an art form.

With a BFA from Savannah College of Art and Design and vast experience in graphic design and Illustration, I’ve been featured in several design industry publications including 2 Illustrator Bibles. I’ve also designed hundreds of posters, billboards and marketing campaigns for companies such as Starz, (like HBO). I’ve had the pleasure of producing work for a wide variety of clients ranging from the Entertainment to the Financial industries and everything in-between.

I love challenges as I see them as an opportunity to learn and inevitably grow. The notion that I will continually discover fresh ideas and share them with others within my career is exhilarating. UX design reflects that mindset as one is faced with continually learning not only about new techniques, technologies, and skills but also about the motivations and struggles of human beings.
